## 2. Key Financial Metrics & Benchmarks (Aligned to TCS Standards)

### Profitability
- Gross Profit Margin: >30%
- Net Profit Margin: >22%
- EBITDA/PBDIT Margin: >31%

### Returns
- ROE: >34%
- ROCE: >24%

### Liquidity
- Current Ratio: >2.0
- Quick Ratio: >1.5

### Leverage
- Debt-to-Equity: <0.25
- Interest Coverage Ratio: >8x

### Efficiency
- Inventory Turnover: >5x
- Receivables Turnover: >8x
- Asset Turnover: >1.6x

## 3. TCS-Specific Evaluation Standards
- Use FITME framework: Fit, Integration, Technology, Management, Execution
- Stress test financials under adverse scenarios
- Use DCF + Comparable Company Valuation
- Benchmark every ratio against TCS & industry averages
- Identify red flags: declining margins, customer concentration, tax litigation, weak cash flow

## 5. Red Flag System Used in Real M&A Workflows

### Category 1: Revenue & Margins Red Flags
- Revenue CAGR < 5%
- Margin erosion for 2‚Äì3 years
- High customer concentration (>35% from 1 client)
- Misalignment between revenue and cash receipts

### Category 2: Cash Flow Red Flags
- Negative operating cash flow
- Cash profits < 50% of EBITDA
- Rapidly increasing credit sales
- High reliance on advances

### Category 3: Debt & Financial Risk Red Flags
- D/E > 1
- Interest coverage < 2x
- High short-term debt dependence
- Unsecured related-party loans

### Category 4: Asset Quality Red Flags
- Overstated intangible assets
- Old machinery requiring replacement
- Large write-offs concealed
- Fake or inflated CapEx entries

### Category 5: Compliance & Governance Red Flags
- Frequent auditor changes
- Statutory dues unpaid
- GST/Income Tax discrepancies
- Heavy related-party transactions

### Category 6: Forecasting Risk Red Flags
- Unrealistic projections
- Forecast margins much higher than historical
- No sensitivity/scenario analysis
- Revenue assumptions not validated

## 6. Deal-Breaker Red Flags (Automatic Disqualification)
- ‚ùå Evidence of fraud
- ‚ùå Auditor qualifications (2+ years)
- ‚ùå Tax or regulatory investigations
- ‚ùå Negative net worth
- ‚ùå Undisclosed liabilities
- ‚ùå >50% reliance on short-term debt
- ‚ùå 20%+ revenue from related parties without contracts
