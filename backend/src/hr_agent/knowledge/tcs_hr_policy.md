## 1. INTRODUCTION AND PRINCIPLES
This HR Policy Manual establishes the human resources practices and procedures of Tata Consultancy Services Limited (TCS). The manual ensures fair, equitable, and compliant employment practices aligned with Indian labor laws, IT sector standards, and Tata Group values.

**Core Principles:**  
- Equal Opportunity  
- Transparency  
- Fairness  
- Dignity  
- Compliance  
- Accountability  

All employees receive this manual during onboarding and acknowledge receipt.

## 2. CODE OF CONDUCT AND WORKPLACE ETHICS

### 2.1 Ethical Standards
Employees must maintain the highest standards of integrity and ethical conduct.

### 2.2 Harassment and Discrimination
Zero tolerance for:  
- Sexual Harassment  
- Bullying  
- Discrimination  

Formal complaints handled by ICC under POSH.

### 2.3 Dress Code
Business casual is required unless client demands otherwise.

### 2.4 Social Media Guidelines
Employees cannot represent TCS without written approval.

### 2.5 Substance Abuse
Strictly prohibited; support available via Wellbeing Programs.

### 2.6 Disciplinary Action
Details on progressive discipline and severe violations.
