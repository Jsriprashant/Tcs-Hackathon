**Parties:** Supernova Motors Inc. ("Lessor") & Urban Rider LLC ("Lessee")

50 e-bikes at $800/month/unit for 36 months. Mileage cap 10K/year.  

## 2. Term & Renewal  
Auto-renews yearly unless 45 days' notice. Escalation: 7% annual. No early exit fee cap.  

## 3. SLAs  
99% uptime; credits limited to 10% monthly fee. Unlimited liability for lessee damages.  

## 4. Resale  
Lessee may sub-lease with consent; no transfer on merger.
