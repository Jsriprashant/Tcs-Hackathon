# BBD Software  Ltd – B2B Partnership & Collaboration Agreement

This Partnership Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into by:

**BBD Software  Ltd**  
and  
**Partner Company:** _______________________________________

## 1. Purpose
This Agreement establishes a collaborative business and technical partnership for joint product development, solution integration, or cooperative service delivery.

## 2. Scope of Collaboration
Collaborations may include:
- Shared or joint software development  
- API or platform integrations  
- Co‑marketing initiatives  
- White‑label or resale agreements  
- Shared engineering or design resources  

Each project will have its own Statement of Work (SOW).

## 3. Responsibilities

### **BBD Software  Ltd Will:**
- Provide engineering, design, or consulting support  
- Maintain high quality and security standards  
- Deliver documentation and integration support  

### **Partner Company Will:**
- Provide business insights and domain expertise  
- Supply required data, API access, or workflows  
- Participate in joint marketing and customer outreach  

## 4. Intellectual Property
- Pre‑existing IP remains with each owner  
- Jointly developed IP is co‑owned unless specified otherwise  
- Licensing terms defined per‑project in the SOW  

## 5. Financial Terms
Compensation models may include:
- Revenue sharing  
- Licensing fees  
- Hourly service charges  
- Subscription or usage-based billing  

All financial terms will be documented in project-specific SOWs.

## 6. Confidentiality
This Agreement is subject to the BBD Software  MNDA.

## 7. Term & Termination
Either party may terminate with 30 days’ notice.  
Obligations regarding confidentiality and unpaid balances survive termination.
