# BBD Software  Ltd – Mutual Non‑Disclosure Agreement (MNDA)

This Mutual Non‑Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between:

**Party 1:** __________________________________  
**BBD Software  Ltd** (“Receiving/Disclosing Party”)  

Both parties may exchange confidential and proprietary information to explore a business relationship.

---

## 1. Definition of Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes:
- Technical data, code, formulas, APIs  
- Business plans, strategies, and forecasts  
- Customer lists and financial details  
- Any orally or visually shared information identified as confidential  

Information is NOT confidential if it:
- Becomes public through no fault of the receiving party  
- Was already known to the receiving party  
- Is disclosed legally by a third party  
- Is independently developed  

---

## 2. Obligations of Receiving Party
The receiving party agrees to:
- Maintain confidentiality using reasonable safeguards  
- Not disclose information to third parties  
- Not use information except to evaluate or perform obligations  
- Not reverse engineer, copy, or export confidential materials  

---

## 3. Return of Materials
Upon request, receiving party must return or destroy all confidential materials.

---

## 4. Legal Compliance
If disclosure is required by law, the receiving party will notify the disclosing party and limit disclosure as allowed.

---

## 5. Term & Enforcement
This Agreement covers disclosures made within 1 year of signing.  
Confidentiality obligations last **five (5) years** from each disclosure.

Due to irreparable harm caused by breaches, the disclosing party is entitled to seek equitable remedies.

---

## 6. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of Delaware, USA.
