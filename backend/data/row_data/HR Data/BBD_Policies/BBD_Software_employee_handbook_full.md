# BBD Software  Ltd – Employee Handbook

Welcome to BBD Software  Ltd. This handbook outlines expectations, benefits, responsibilities, and operational procedures for employees, contractors, and partners.

---

## 1. Expectations for Employees
Employees are expected to uphold professionalism, integrity, and transparency. You must:
- Deliver high‑quality work  
- Maintain secure, updated systems  
- Communicate clearly with colleagues and clients  
- Respect confidentiality and data privacy  
- Treat others with kindness and collaboration  

## 2. Part-Time Employees
Part-time employees follow the same expectations but receive modified hours and benefit structures based on their employment contracts.

## 3. Expectations for Contractors
Contractors contribute essential expertise to BBD Software . Contractors must:
- Produce professional-grade work  
- Follow BBD Software  processes for documentation and version control  
- Send daily status updates  
- Participate in meetings when required  
- Communicate promptly during working hours  

Contractors receive perks including:
- Coffee benefits  
- Friday lunches or stipends  
- Invitations to company events  

---

## 4. Compensation & Payroll

### **Salaries**
Compensation is based on market benchmarks, role, and experience.

### **Performance Reviews**
Conducted twice a year; may result in raises or bonuses.

### **Bonuses**
Discretionary and dependent on company performance.

### **Payroll Cycle**
Salaries are deposited twice monthly.

### **Contractor Invoicing**
- Contractors submit invoices bi-weekly  
- Payment terms: Net 30  
- Invoices sent to: accounts@BBD Softwaresoft.com  

---

## 5. Communications & Operations

### Tools and Policies
- Company email provided to all employees  
- Slack or Teams used for internal communication  
- Jira/Asana for project management  
- Mandatory use of password managers  
- Shared calendars for scheduling  
- Daily standups at 10 AM  
- Weekly project check-ins and Friday wrap-up  

### Core Hours
All employees must be available from **10 AM – 4 PM**.

---

## 6. Policies & Procedures

### Non-Discrimination
BBD Software  prohibits discrimination based on race, gender, religion, disability, age, or other protected categories.

### Anti‑Harassment
Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.

### Grievance Procedure
Employees may report concerns confidentially to HR. Issues are investigated promptly.

### Disciplinary Procedure
Progressive steps include:
1. Verbal warning  
2. Written warning  
3. Suspension  
4. Termination  

### Labor Compliance
No child labor or forced labor is permitted in any part of our operations.

### Whistleblower Protection
Employees who report violations are protected from retaliation.

---

## 7. Benefits

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees receive:
- 15 days PTO annually  
- PTO payout upon termination  

### Holidays
10 paid holidays per year.

### Parental Leave
Up to 12 weeks maternity leave and 2 weeks paternity leave.

### Professional Development
Budgets available for training, certifications, and conferences.

### Technology Benefits
Company laptop refreshed every 3 years.

### Food & Drink
- Friday lunches  
- Office snacks and beverage perks  

---

## 8. Acknowledgement
By signing your employment contract, you acknowledge and agree to follow the policies in this handbook.
