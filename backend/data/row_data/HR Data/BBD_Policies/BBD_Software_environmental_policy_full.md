# BBD Software  Ltd – Environmental Sustainability Policy (v1.0)

## Purpose
BBD Software  Ltd recognizes that technology companies have a meaningful environmental footprint driven by cloud computing, device usage, and operational energy consumption. We commit to minimizing our impact through responsible resource use, sustainable procurement, and environmental awareness across our organization.

## 1. Understanding Our Impact
To make meaningful improvements, BBD Software  evaluates:
- Cloud hosting energy consumption  
- Office electricity and water usage  
- Employee commuting and travel emissions  
- Electronic waste generation  
- Vendor and supply-chain sustainability  

We maintain internal reporting to help guide measurable sustainability decisions.

## 2. Reducing Environmental Impact
We strive to reduce our footprint through:

### **Energy Efficiency**
- Use LED lighting, energy‑efficient appliances, and automation tools in office spaces  
- Prefer renewable‑energy‑powered cloud service providers  
- Encourage remote work to reduce commuting emissions  

### **Water Conservation**
- Install water-efficient fixtures  
- Perform monthly usage evaluations  
- Participate in municipal conservation programs  

### **Waste Reduction**
- Compost all organic waste where possible  
- Recycle electronics, batteries, and plastics using certified facilities  
- Offer e‑waste drop‑off days for employees  

### **Procurement Standards**
We prioritize:
- Vendors with environmental certifications  
- Locally sourced goods to reduce transportation emissions  
- Companies owned by women, minorities, and environmentally responsible founders  

## 3. Community & Team Engagement
BBD Software  promotes sustainability by:
- Educating employees about responsible digital and physical resource usage  
- Sharing tools, templates, and best practices with partners  
- Supporting local environmental programs  

## 4. Continuous Commitment
Sustainability is an evolving goal. BBD Software  will periodically review and update this policy to reflect new technologies, regulations, and organizational practices.
