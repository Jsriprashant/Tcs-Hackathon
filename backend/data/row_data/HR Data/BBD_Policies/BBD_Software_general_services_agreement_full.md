# BBD Software  Ltd – General Services Agreement (GSA)

This Independent Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of  
**(Effective Date)** between:

**Client:** ____________________________  
**BBD Software  Ltd** (“BBD Software”), a software development and technology services company.

This Agreement defines the terms under which BBD Software  delivers software, engineering, consulting, and technology services to the Client. Both parties agree to collaborate professionally and transparently to achieve project success.

---

## 1. Engagement

### **1.1 Definitions**
Key definitions appear in Section 10 of this Agreement.

### **1.2 Performance of Services**
BBD Software  will perform services described in one or more Statements of Work or Engagement Agreements (“Engagement Agreements”). Each Engagement Agreement incorporates this Agreement and supersedes conflicting terms.

BBD Software  maintains autonomy over how work is executed and may subcontract specialists when necessary.

### **1.3 Change Requests**
Clients may request changes (“Change Proposals”). BBD Software  will evaluate feasibility, budget impact, and timeline adjustments. Changes take effect only after written approval by both parties.

### **1.4 Competitive Engagements**
BBD Software  may work with competitors unless otherwise agreed.

### **1.5 Independent Contractor**
BBD Software  acts as an independent contractor. This Agreement does not create an employment, partnership, or joint venture relationship.

---

## 2. Compensation

### **2.1 Fees**
Fees are defined in each Engagement Agreement. Pre-approved expenses will be reimbursed.

### **2.2 Responsibility for Momentum**
Both parties share responsibility for project momentum.  
Delays caused by Client (e.g., missing approvals, data, or access) may result in compensation of up to **$500 per full day** of obstruction.

---

## 3. Confidentiality

Each party must protect the other’s Confidential Information using reasonable care.  
Confidentiality obligations survive this Agreement indefinitely.

---

## 4. Deliverables

### **4.1 Ownership**
Upon full payment, all Deliverables become the Client’s property.  
BBD Software  retains ownership of pre-existing IP (“BBD Software  IP”). BBD Software  grants a **perpetual, royalty-free license** for any BBD Software  IP embedded in Deliverables.

### **4.2 Portfolio Use**
BBD Software  may use non-sensitive work for marketing and educational purposes.

### **4.3 Acceptance**
The Client has **five (5) business days** after delivery to report issues. Lack of response constitutes acceptance.

---

## 8. Termination

- Either party may terminate with **30 days’ written notice**, or immediately for material breach.  
- Upon termination, BBD Software  will deliver all completed or in-progress materials, and Client will pay all outstanding amounts.

---

## 9. General Provisions

- **Governing Law:** Delaware, USA  
- **Notices:** Must be written and delivered via email, courier, or certified mail  
- **Legal Fees:** Prevailing party in litigation may recover attorney fees  
- **Entire Agreement:** This document supersedes all prior understandings  
- **Amendments:** Must be in writing and signed by both parties  

---

## 10. Definitions

- **Confidential Information:** Business, technical, or strategic information not publicly known  
- **Deliverables:** All work products produced for the Client  
- **BBD Software  IP:** Pre-existing or independently developed intellectual property of BBD Software   
- **Services:** Activities defined in Engagement Agreements
