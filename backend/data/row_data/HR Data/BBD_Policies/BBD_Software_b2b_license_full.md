# BBD Software  Ltd – B2B Services & Licensing Policy

This policy defines the standards and structures through which BBD Software  Ltd provides B2B software, support, and licensing services.

---

## 1. Service Categories
BBD Software  offers:
- SaaS platform subscriptions  
- Custom enterprise deployments  
- API access licensing (Free / Standard / Enterprise tiers)  
- Dedicated support engineering packages  
- Long-term maintenance contracts  

---

## 2. Licensing Models

### **Subscription Licensing**
- Monthly or annual commitments  
- Usage or seat‑based pricing  

### **Enterprise Licensing**
- On‑premise deployments available  
- Includes security audits and compliance certification  

### **API Licensing**
Tier-based access may include:
- Rate limits  
- Premium endpoints  
- Dedicated support  

---

## 3. Support Levels

### **Standard Support**
- Response time: 48 hours  

### **Premium Support**
- Response time: 24 hours  
- Monthly performance reviews  

### **Enterprise Support**
- Response time: 4 hours  
- Dedicated account manager  
- Quarterly security & performance audits  

---

## 4. Compliance
BBD Software  aligns with:
- GDPR  
- ISO security best practices  
- SOC2-compatible operational controls  
- Global data protection regulations  

---

## 5. Renewals & Termination
- Contracts renew automatically unless canceled 30 days before renewal date  
- Early termination terms defined in the master contract  

---

## 6. Modifications
BBD Software  reserves the right to update licensing policies with 30 days' written notice.
