
# Supernova Motors – B2B Services & Licensing Policy

Supernova licenses EV drivetrain software, ADAS modules, mobility‑platform APIs, and telematics analytics to OEMs, fleet operators, and city transit agencies.

## 1. Licensing Categories

### ADAS Licensing
- Lane‑centering  
- Traffic‑jam assist  
- Sensor‑fusion models  
- Over‑the‑air updates for autonomy modules  

### Mobility Platform Licensing
- Dispatch engines  
- Battery‑health analytics  
- Predictive maintenance scoring  
- Fleet optimization algorithms  

## 2. Pricing Models
- Annual subscription  
- Per‑vehicle licensing  
- Per‑fleet‑size tiering  
- Compute‑usage billing for cloud inference  

## 3. Support
- Standard: 72‑hour response  
- Premium: 24‑hour response  
- Enterprise: dedicated TAM + quarterly audits  

## 4. Compliance
GDPR, ISO 26262, ISO 21448 (SOTIF), SOC2 compatible.

## 5. Renewal
Auto‑renews unless canceled 45 days prior.
