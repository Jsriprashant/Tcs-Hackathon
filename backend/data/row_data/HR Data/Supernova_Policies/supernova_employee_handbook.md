
# Supernova Motors – Employee Handbook

Supernova Motors builds EVs, hybrids, ADAS technology, and mobility‑services platforms. Our teams include hardware engineers, autonomy researchers, data scientists, fleet‑ops specialists, field technicians, and corporate staff.

## 1. Expectations
Employees must:
- Maintain secure engineering practices  
- Protect ADAS and EV proprietary data  
- Deliver work aligned with program deadlines  
- Foster teamwork across hardware‑software teams  

## 2. Contractors
Contractors follow similar obligations and receive:
- Facility access  
- Coffee & meal stipends  
- Participation in relevant R&D reviews  

## 3. Compensation
- Salaries reviewed annually  
- Bonus tied to vehicle‑program milestones  
- Payroll twice monthly  
- Contractors: net‑30 invoices  

## 4. Communications
- Supernova email required  
- Slack + Jira for engineering workflows  
- Daily standup by functional unit  
- Core hours: 10am–4pm  

## 5. Policies
- Non‑discrimination  
- Anti‑harassment  
- Whistleblower protections  
- Family‑Friendly Manifesto  
- Environmental Policy  
- Ethical Marketing Policy  

## 6. Benefits
- Health, dental, vision  
- PTO: 18 days annually  
- 12 weeks parental leave  
- Technology refresh every 3 years  
- Free EV charging for employees at campuses
