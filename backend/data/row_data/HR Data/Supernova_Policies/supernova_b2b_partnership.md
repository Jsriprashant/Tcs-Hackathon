
# Supernova Motors – B2B Partnership & Collaboration Agreement

**Partner:** Orion AutoTech Ltd.  
**Supernova Motors, Inc.**

## 1. Purpose
To jointly develop EV platforms, ADAS algorithms, and integrated mobility‑service offerings.

## 2. Areas of Collaboration
- Shared autonomy research  
- Joint sensor‑calibration datasets  
- Co‑developed fleet‑management software  
- White‑label mobility solutions for municipal clients  

## 3. Responsibilities

### Supernova
- Provide engineering resources, simulation infrastructure, and telematics APIs  
- Maintain cybersecurity and regulatory compliance  

### Partner
- Provide market access, vehicle platforms, operational data, and deployment support  

## 4. IP Ownership
Jointly developed IP is co‑owned unless otherwise specified. Pre-existing IP remains with each party.

## 5. Financial Structure
- Revenue‑share models for co‑built platforms  
- Engineering fees for dedicated work  
- Cloud‑compute pass‑through charges  

## 6. Term & Termination
30‑day notice; confidentiality and outstanding payments survive termination.
