
# Supernova Motors – Mutual Non‑Disclosure Agreement

**Parties:** Supernova Motors, Inc. and [External Partner]

## 1. Definition of Confidential Information
Includes EV schematics, ADAS models, ML datasets, sensor‑fusion libraries, fleet‑mobility analytics, financial documents, and customer data.

## 2. Obligations
Receiving Party must:
- Protect information with reasonable safeguards  
- Not disclose to third parties  
- Not reverse engineer, copy, or export restricted data  
- Not use information beyond agreed evaluation  

Exceptions: publicly known data, independently developed info, or lawful disclosures.

## 3. Return of Materials
Upon request, all confidential data—including firmware, training sets, logs—must be returned or securely deleted.

## 4. Term
Disclosure window: 1 year  
Confidentiality duration: 5 years after each disclosure

## 5. Governing Law
Delaware, USA.
